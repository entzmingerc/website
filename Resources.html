Synthstrom Deluge Resources

Deluge Tips and Tricks Document!

On the Deluge Discord, I started an FAQ document that quickly grew into a large document full of all the tips and tricks. Over a few months I added tips that I knew and ones that I could find shared on the discord and other forums. Other community members contributed as well and it's grown to be a great resource for those learning the Deluge.


Deluge Community Synth Presets!

There are a lot of synth presets shared across the discord, forum, and other social media. I created a GitHub repo to consolidate all the presets I could find on the internet into a single location. Preset packs that I didn't know if I could share freely are linked to in the README. Hopefully in the future, we'll make a Community SD Card image full of synth and kit presets sourced entirely from Deluge users.


Discord Emojis

I've created dozens of emojis for the Synthstrom Deluge Discord server and the Monome Crow Discord server.
